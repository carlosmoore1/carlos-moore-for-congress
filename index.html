About the Candidate

A career built on advocacy and accountability

Background

  • JD Florida State University College of Law
  • BA University of South Alabama
  • HS Moss Point High School
  • Role Civil rights attorney
  • Role Former municipal judge, Mississippi
  • Leadership Past President, National Bar Association (2021–2022)

Judge Carlos E. Moore has spent his career in courtrooms and communities, building a reputation as a civil rights attorney who has represented high-profile clients and pushed for accountability in cases of police misconduct.

He served as President of the National Bar Association from 2021 to 2022, and has been active in civic and youth development work, including founding a local chapter of 100 Black Men and supporting scholarship programs for students.

Now running as a Democrat for Georgia's 13th Congressional District, Moore says his campaign is about bringing a fighter's energy and a lawyer's precision to Washington.
